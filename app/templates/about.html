About UFuel

UFuel is a nutrition-focused web application designed to help students meet their dietary goals while navigating busy academic schedules.

Our Mission

UFuel simplifies nutrition for students who need quick, reliable, and personalized meal recommendations. Using user input such as demographics, goals, and dietary restrictions, the system generates tailored meal plans built from a curated and maintainable food library.

Who We Designed It For

UFuel is built for Towson University students and staff who want quick, simple, and personalized nutrition guidance. Whether you’re grabbing food between classes or planning healthier habits, UFuel helps you make better choices with zero stress.
